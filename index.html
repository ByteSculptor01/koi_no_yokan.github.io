About Me

I am an engineer at the intersection of computer architecture, embedded systems, and robotics, with a passion for designing high-performance, energy-efficient hardware that powers the future of AI, IoT, and autonomous systems. My work bridges the gap between hardware and software, pushing the limits of custom computing architectures, intelligent motor control, and embedded AI applications.

Currently pursuing graduate studies in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park, I specialize in computer systems, architecture, and embedded systems. My journey began with cutting-edge research at IIT Gandhinagar and has led me to develop firmware and custom hardware solutions for autonomous robots.

Over the years, I've collaborated with both industry leaders and academic research teams, honing my skills in embedded system design, sensor integration, and control algorithms. I've tackled complex engineering challenges and innovated within embedded AI and system design, creating reliable, future-proof solutions.
